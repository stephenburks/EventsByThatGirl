Services

What We Do

Events by That Girl offers a variety of services that can be catered to fit anyone’s needs. We currently specialize in day-of coordinating for events, but can also do partial and full event planning. We also offer consultations if you just need a little help planning your own event! The first step for any new client is a quick meeting and questionnaire to get an idea of what you are looking for and potential dates for your event!

"With Samantha's diverse background in the hospitality industry, she has what it takes to plan every type of event. From weddings to family gatherings, even corperate events, she does it all!"

After that, we’ll work together to figure out exactly what you need and what we will be able to offer. Whether you’re planning your dream wedding, a family reunion, or a fabulous holiday party you’ll want Events by That Girl to help make your event perfect!

Our Service Packages

"Most Of It" Package

Our “Most of It” Package is an ideal option for the couple who may have already completed a portion of their planning and require less assistance than our “Everything” package offers. This package is designed to allow the couple a hands-on role in their planning activities with our professional assistance to guide them along the way.

Planning

  • Email and text message assistance for wedding etiquette, planning advice, vendor ideas, and any other questions
  • Up to 12 hours of in-person, phone, or video chat meetings throughout the wedding planning process, scheduled in advance based upon mutual availability. *Additional meetings may be scheduled for an hourly fee
  • Customized referrals to match you with the very best wedding professionals for your budget, style, and personality
  • A custom planning checklist that will provide you with guidance along your journey
  • Act as a liaison between you and your vendors to ensure everything is in order
  • Assistance with hotel accommodations and setting up room blocks for guest
  • A comprehensive timeline for the wedding day
  • A plan for all event set up and tear down coordinated with the other vendors and venue
  • Confirmation with vendors for arrival times and fulfillment of contract & expectations
  • Attendance and direction (with officiant) at ceremony rehearsal (2-hour maximum)
  • Assistance with pick up and delivery of any last minute wedding items or details

Day-Of-Assistance

  • A full wedding emergency kit with everything you might need for your event
  • Coordinate with vendors on any rental items for the ceremony or reception including delivery and pick up after the event
  • Manage the timeline and execution of the ceremony
    • Arrival at the venue prior to any vendors to greet them and make sure they have everything they need
    • Set up simple decor at the ceremony & reception. For example; programs, escort cards, place cards, photos, menu cards, guest book, cake cutting utensils, champagne flutes, etc.
    • Assist with ceremony flowers, helping to pin on boutonnieres and hand out corsages & bouquets
    • Lighting candles at the ceremony in a timely fashion
    • Instruct the host, hostess, ushers, and other attendants as necessary
    • Manage wedding party and coordinate their walk down the aisle ensuring flowers are held correctly, timing is impeccable, and that all attire is in order
  • Assist photographer/videographer to capture any requested poses and the overall feel of your wedding day, including placement of wedding party and family members
  • Assist with transportation of items that may need to be taken to the reception site (restrictions may apply)
  • Manage the timeline and execution of all reception events
    • Coordinate the grand entrance of the bride & groom
    • Set up of any special items requested by the bride & groom at the ceremony and reception
    • Assistance with the bustling of the wedding gown
    • Coordinate with caterer and entertainment for toasts, dances, cake cutting, etc.
    • Ensure the bride & groom are able to enjoy their meal.
    • Coordinate the collection of gifts and keepsakes received at the wedding
    • Manage final payments and close out with vendors
