About

Events By That Girl

What We Do

Samantha Heaton, the owner and creative genius of Events by That Girl has been a part of the Aiken community for 30 years and part of the event and catering business for over 15 years. Over the years she has had experience working with a variety of clients on many different types of events such as; wedding, anniversary, family reunions, corporate events, holiday parties, fundraisers and more.

Samantha says “The thing I truly love about events is being a part of making memories. I am always doing the unexpected and going above and beyond for my clients to make their dreams come true. I have a knack for knowing the clients needs before they arise. I can foresee potential problems and fix them before they happen.”

Events by That Girl offers a variety of services that can be catered to fit anyone’s needs. We currently specialize in day-of coordinating for events, but can also do partial and full event planning. We also offer consultations if you just need a little help planning your own event! The first step for any new client is a quick meeting and questionnaire to get an idea of what you are looking for and potential dates for your event! After that, we’ll work together to figure out exactly what you need and what we will be able to offer. Whether you’re planning your dream wedding, a family reunion, or a fabulous holiday party you’ll want Events by That Girl to help make your event perfect!
