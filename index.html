Events By That Girls

Samantha Heaton, the owner and creative genius of Events by That Girl has been a part of the Aiken community for 30 years and part of the event and catering business for over 15 years. Over the years she has had experience working with a variety of clients on many different types of events such as; wedding, anniversary, family reunions, corporate events, holiday parties, fundraisers and more.

Samantha says “The thing I truly love about events is being a part of making memories. I am always doing the unexpected and going above and beyond for my clients to make their dreams come true. I have a knack for knowing the clients needs before they arise. I can foresee potential problems and fix them before they happen.”
